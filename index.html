Mobile application Sfera

Problem

As a residential real estate developer, the client wanted to enhance the living experience in their newly built complexes through digital innovation. Prior to the app, there was no unified way for residents to manage utilities, request services, or engage with the management team in one place. This lack of infrastructure led to fragmented communication, manual processes, and limited visibility into resident needs.

Solution

Our team took part in developing of the Sfera Mobile App, a comprehensive solution designed to simplify property management for both businesses and individuals. The app provides a range of features tailored to streamline administrative tasks and enhance user experience, such as centralized property management, utility charges and payments, utility requests management and comprehensive analytics and reports.

Used Technologies:

Flutter
Android
IOS
MongoDB

Result

Website redesign (Epicentr K)

Problem

The client’s existing website was not effectively supporting their brand or business goals. The hero section failed to capture attention, calls to action were missing or unclear, and the overall content layout was poorly optimized for user experience. As a result, visitors struggled to engage with the site or take meaningful actions, which negatively impacted lead generation and brand perception.

Solution

Our team conducted a comprehensive audit of the client’s previous website and uncovered key usability and engagement issues, including an ineffective hero section, lack of calls to action, and poorly structured content. We then delivered a complete redesign that not only resolved these issues but also aligned perfectly with the brand’s tone of voice, resulting in a more engaging and conversion-oriented experience.
